Story of the Ink

I had got a year's work of supply for the ink of Fountain Pen.
When the times had become uncertain and difficult to process, I would tell myself a claim.
I should hold out and keep patience till the supply of ink refills exist.

It was a canary in the mine, helping me find out different ways to thrive and improve.

An Ink refill became the source of sustainable drive, to continue surviving.
